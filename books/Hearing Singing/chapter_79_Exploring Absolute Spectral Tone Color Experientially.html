EXPLORING ABSOLUTE SPECTRAL TONE COLOR EXPERIENTIALLY The basic idea of ASTC is challenging to study scientifically. Play a series of pure tones to naive listeners and you will invariably get a variety of responses to the question ‚ÄúWhat vowel is this like?‚Äù This is to be expected, as few listeners have the grounding to understand this question in terms of the ASTC framework.
