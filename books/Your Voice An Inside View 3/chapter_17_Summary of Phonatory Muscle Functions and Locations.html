laryngeal innervation

superior laryngeal nerve

innervation of cricothyroid muscle; sensation of a tickle in the throat; control of pitch elevation

branches off from the Vagus (10th Cranial) nerve in the neck, slightly above the larynx

recurrent laryngeal nerve

innervation of all other laryngeal muscles; control of adduction, abduction, low pitches

branches off from the Vagus (10th Cranial) nerve below the larynx in the thoracic cavity (left recurrent loops below the aorta)
